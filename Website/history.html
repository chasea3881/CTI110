History of the Boston Terrier

Boston Terriers came from Boston, Massachusetts — that’s where they got their name!

They were accepted by the American Kennel Club in 1893 and have been popular ever since.

Some famous Boston Terrier owners include President Gerald Ford and Helen Keller.

The oldest known Boston Terrier lived to be 15 years old!
