Cost and Benefits of Boston Terriers

Boston Terriers usually cost $600–$1,200 to buy, depending on the breeder or rescue.

Food costs about $30–$50 per month. Vet visits can add up to $300–$500 each year.

But the benefits are worth it! Dogs like Boston Terriers can help you feel happier and even live longer.

I have one of my own, and I can say life just feels better with a Boston Terrier around.
